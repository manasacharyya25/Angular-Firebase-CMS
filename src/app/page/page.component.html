Message From Headmistress
“Education is not the learning of facts, but the training of the mind to think.”
-Albert Einstein

Welcome to the website of Air Force School Chabua. As the headmistress of the school, I feel honoured and privileged to be a part of an educational institution where we work at implementing a well balanced curriculum to ensure that the children who walk into our school will not just love their school years but truly be prepared to face life’s challenges. With the onset of the 21st century, the entire world has witnessed an era of intense transformation in all areas. Naturally, for such times, a different skill set that is 21st century skills are required that would enable an individual to cope up and succeed in facing the challenges in real life, leading to his/her holistic progress. We aim to empower our students with these skills so that they can develop holistically and contribute to the progress and development of society, nation and world.

Learning is complete and holistic only when a student is able to effectively perform and fulfil his/her responsibilities and duties towards self, school, family, society and above all, the nation. So our goal is to enable our students to be a good citizen and a responsible human being who is well aware of his potential and competence.

We have a team of highly dedicated teachers. The basic objective of our teachers is to improve the learning outcomes of the students. We strive to create inclusive classroom environment where all students, irrespective of their individual abilities and skills are welcomed holistically. Here students are encouraged and motivated to take part in a variety of thinking activities, strategies and group dynamics so that the classroom becomes alive.

Parental involvement is a significant factor in student’s progress. We seek parents’ support to know more about the child so that we can work together on the child’s strengths and help him/her in the journey to discover himself/herself. Together lets teach them the important values like honesty, kindness, generosity, humility, optimism, perseverance, tolerance and more so that they can create a beautiful world around them. Together we can make a difference!

A committed and supportive management, dedicated teachers, caring and co-operative parents blend harmoniously to create a child centric school. I am very sure through collaborative effort we can achieve more to benefit our students who are the future leaders of tomorrow.

With sincere and warm regards,
Nuran Hussain
Headmistress
